About Us

Our Story

From a Single Site to a Multi-Sector Contractor

Bejundas Construction grew out of Bejundas Group's vision of building a diversified Tanzanian holdings business. What started with residential builds in Dar es Salaam has expanded into civil works across the lakes regions, commercial fit-outs in Dodoma's growing administrative quarter, and industrial facilities serving the country's logistics corridor.

Every project we take on is treated as a long-term partnership. We don't disappear after handover — we stand behind our work with structured warranties, scheduled inspections, and an in-house maintenance team for repeat clients.

Our Mission

Deliver every project on spec, on time, and on budget — without cutting corners.

We treat client capital and timelines as if they were our own. That means realistic schedules over optimistic ones, transparent change orders, and weekly progress reports backed by site photography.

Our Vision

Become Tanzania's most-trusted private-sector contractor by 2030.

Trust isn't built by marketing. It's built by finishing what you started — and being there when something needs fixing five years later. That's the standard we measure ourselves against.
