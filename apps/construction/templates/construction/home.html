Who We Are

A Construction Partner Built on Trust, Engineered for Tanzania

Bejundas Construction is the construction arm of Bejundas Group of Companies — a multidisciplinary contractor delivering design-build, civil works, MEP installation, and project management services across mainland Tanzania and Zanzibar. We work with private developers, institutional clients, and government agencies to bring complex projects from drawing board to handover.

Our approach combines local market expertise with international quality standards — every site we run is registered with the NCC, supervised by certified engineers, and delivered against agreed timelines and budgets.
