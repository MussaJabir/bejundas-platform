About Us

Tanzanian Farms, Honest Yield.

Our Story

Built on Land, Grown by Hand.

BEJUNDAS FARMING LTD is the agribusiness arm of Bejundas Group of Companies — producing everyday staples and poultry for the Tanzanian market. We grow crops, raise birds, and supply wholesalers, retailers, restaurants, and institutional buyers.

Our model is straightforward: own working land, run it well, and keep the supply chain short. Buyers deal with the producer directly — no middlemen, no opaque pricing, no surprises.

What we focus on

  • 1
    Staple crops — maize, sunflower, beans, vegetables, fruits.
  • 2
    Poultry — layer eggs, broilers, and kienyeji birds.
  • 3
    Processed goods — sunflower oil and related farm output.
  • 4
    Wholesale and partnerships — bulk supply contracts and contract farming relationships.
