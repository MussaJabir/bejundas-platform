About Us

A Tanzanian Financial Services Provider, Privately Held.

Who We Are

Built on Salama, Faida, Uaminifu.

BEJUNDAS FINANCIAL SERVICES LTD is a privately-held Tanzanian limited company headquartered at Msakuzi - Mbezi, North Local Government Offices, Ubungo Municipality in Dar es Salaam.

We are a financial services provider funding mixed business ventures including mobile network & banking agency, banking branch franchising, mutual-fund investments, government treasury bills and bonds, stock market business at the Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange, microfinancing (personal, corporate, and group loans) at favourable and affordable rates with negotiable repayment schedules, and refinancing against retail businesses.

Our Values

  • Salama — Safety in how we manage capital, partnerships, and client relationships.
  • Faida — Profitability for our clients and the businesses we fund.
  • Uaminifu — Trust earned through transparency and consistent delivery.
