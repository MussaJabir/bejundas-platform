What You Can Do Right Now

No Code Needed
1
Replace any project with real content Edit any of the 10 seeded projects in the admin — change title, description, location, year, upload a real cover image. Changes appear on the site instantly.
2
Add new projects, services, testimonials Use the "Construction" group in the admin sidebar. Each section has its own form. New entries appear in the right place on the site automatically — no developer required.
3
Receive quote requests via email Anyone filling the quote form sends a notification to your leads email. The submission also appears in the admin where you can move it through your workflow (Reviewed → Quoted → Won/Lost).
4
Feature your best projects on the homepage Tick the "Featured" checkbox on a project, and it appears on the homepage. Untick it, and it goes back to just the portfolio page. Simple as that.
5
Manage incoming RFP attachments When a client uploads plans or sketches with their quote request, those files are attached to the record in the admin — viewable, downloadable, archived alongside the submission.
