What You Can Do Right Now

No Code Needed
1
Edit any service line Go to Admin → Financial → Services. Change the name, summary, description, icon, or category. The site updates instantly.
2
Launch a new investment round Admin → Financial → Investment Offerings → Add. Set the reference (e.g. BFS/IO/2026/03), rate, tenure, min capital, opens/closes/settlement dates, and tick "Featured" to surface it on the homepage.
3
Work your inquiry pipelines Loan Inquiries and Investment Inquiries each have their own workflow with bulk actions. Move applications through the stages (Reviewed, Approved, Disbursed) or rounds (Contacted, Committed, Funded, Active, Matured).
4
Receive branded email notifications Every loan application and investment inquiry triggers a navy/gold branded email to your leads inbox — with all the form data formatted properly and a "Review in Admin" button that takes you straight to the record.
5
Replace placeholder testimonials Edit the 4 seeded testimonials with real client quotes as they come in. Tick "Featured" on the best three to surface them on the homepage.
6
Add regulator badges when supplied The Certifications section is empty by design — when you have a BoT registration number, CMSA filing, or BRELA reference to display, add it as a Certification entry and it appears on the About page.

Built on Trust, Profit, Safety

Salama · Faida · Uaminifu isn't just a tagline — it's wired into the experience. Regulatory disclaimer on every page, indicative-not-guaranteed wording on every offering, no false promises in the copy. The site is your front-of-house — its tone matches your business reality.

Compliance posture.
