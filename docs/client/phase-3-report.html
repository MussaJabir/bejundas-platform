Every Sub-Site Captures Visitor Enquiries

Each business address has a "Get Notified" form. When a visitor fills in their name and email, their enquiry is recorded in the admin panel and delivered to your inbox by email — with a note showing which business they are interested in.

Each Business Has Its Own Colour Identity

The colour scheme for each sub-site is stored in the admin panel and can be changed at any time without a developer. Financial uses navy and gold, Construction uses charcoal and orange, and so on — each one looking distinct and appropriate for its industry.

Secure By Design

When a visitor submits an enquiry from the Financial sub-site, the system records it as a Financial lead — even if someone tried to manipulate the form. The business category is always confirmed from the web address, not from what the visitor types.
