Why This Is Better

Reasons
1
Stronger group identity All businesses share the same Bejundas address. Visitors immediately understand they're inside one trusted group, not on six unrelated websites.
2
Easier to share & remember "Visit us at bejundas.co.tz/farming" is much easier to say in a meeting, write on a business card, or print on a billboard than farming.bejundas.co.tz.
3
Better Google ranking When everything is on one address, every visitor and every link helps the whole brand rise on Google searches. Spread across six addresses, that strength is divided up.
4
Cleaner setup, fewer moving parts One website, one security certificate, one set of settings. Less to maintain, fewer things that can go wrong, faster to make changes.

What this means for your visitors

For someone visiting the site, nothing important changes — every business page still looks and behaves the same, with its own colours, content, and contact form. Just a tidier web address at the top of their browser. Old subdomain addresses are no longer in use; visitors should now use the new addresses listed above when sharing or promoting any specific business.

Live and ready to share

The new addresses are live now. Use them on business cards, social media, brochures, and email signatures going forward.
